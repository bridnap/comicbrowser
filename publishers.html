List of Publishers

DC Comics

The home of iconic super heroes such as Superman, Batman, and the Flash

Marvel Comics

The home of Spider-Man, the X-men, and the Black Cat! Oh and Stan Lee!

Dark Horse Comics

The place where movies and pop culture come to comics. Star Wars and Predator came to comics at Dark Horse. Buffy the Vampire Slayer a little later.

Image Comics

The creatives left Marvel and DC, starting their own publishing company. Spawn was the first and the best. Youngblood, Savage Dragon, and Witchblade came soon after.
