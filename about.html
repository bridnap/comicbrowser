A Warm Welcome!

The purpose of this website is to provide details on character and super hero team first appearances in the comic book world. It’s a project I’ve actually had in mind for several years, but never truly implemented it.

My target audience are comic book enthusiasts, but more specifically the type of comic book enthusiasts that wants more detail than story. I also don’t want to spend a lot of time on main characters like Spider-man, Superman, and Batman. The focus should be more on minor characters, or characters that play a significant role, but are not as well known.
